About MangaLore

WHERE FANDOM MEETS CHAIN

MangaLore was built on a simple belief: manga fans deserve more than passive consumption.

Powered by elizaOS for agent intelligence and built on Solana for fast, near-free on-chain transactions — MangaLore is the first AI companion that turns your manga knowledge into real on-chain rewards.

We pull from AniList, MangaDex, and Jikan — covering 18,000+ titles — so your favorite series, however niche, is already here waiting for you.
