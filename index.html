How To Use

Start in
60 Seconds

No wallet required to begin. Connect when you're ready to claim rewards.

01
Step One
Open MangaLore on Telegram

Find us on Telegram and hit Start. MangaLore will greet you as your personal AI manga companion and walk you through the four core experiences.

@mangaloreagent_bot → /start
02
Step Two
Take Your First Daily Quest

Pick any manga or let MangaLore surprise you. Choose your difficulty — Easy warms you up, Legendary tests true mastery. You have 30 seconds per question.

/quiz → pick manga → answer A/B/C/D
03
Step Three
Explore Alternative Universes

Pick a manga, name a scene you wish had gone differently, and watch the AI rewrite history in 500 words — staying true to the lore you love.

/whatif → manga title → pick scene
04
Step Four
Connect Wallet & Claim Rewards

Link your Solana wallet to convert LORE Points to LORE Tokens, mint your NFT character card, and receive SOL when you reach the monthly leaderboard top 10.

/wallet → connect Solana → claim

About MangaLore

Where Fandom
Meets the Chain

MangaLore was born from a simple belief: manga fans deserve more than passive consumption. The stories we love shape how we think, feel, and see the world — and that passion should be celebrated, challenged, and rewarded.

Built on Solana for speed and low-cost transactions, powered by elizaOS for agent intelligence, and fueled by open manga databases including AniList, MangaDex, and Jikan — MangaLore is the first AI companion that turns your knowledge into real on-chain rewards.

We are not a platform. We are a companion. One that grows with you, learns your taste, and lives inside the app you already use every day.
